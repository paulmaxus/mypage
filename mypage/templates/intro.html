Max Paulus

I'm a Computational Linguist / Cognitive Scientist, researching Speech and Hearing at the Faculty of Psychology and Language Sciences (University College London). In my PhD, I use physiological measures such as the pupil size to understand how humans adapt to degraded speech. Adapting to spectrally degraded speech is a fundamental mechanism that allows users of a cochlear implant to hear again. In the long run, I want to use my background in NLP to create assistive technologies that are grounded in my experimental research.
